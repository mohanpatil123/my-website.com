About Me

Hello Nice to meet you,

My name is Mohan Patil, and I proudly carry the title of a software developer . My journey into the realm of technology began with my diploma at Government Polytechnic in Murtizapur , a journey that laid the foundation for what was to come. Recently, I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering , a chapter filled with exploration and discovery.

During my studies, I immersed myself in a multitude of technologies, each one adding a layer to my understanding of the digital landscape. From the foundational languages like C , to the web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript , I delved deep into the intricacies of software development. I honed my skills in frameworks like Bootstrap , dabbled in the realms of Java and its ecosystem , and explored the world of Data Structures and Algorithms.

But learning goes beyond the classroom. It's in the projects we undertake, the challenges we face, and the solutions we craft.
